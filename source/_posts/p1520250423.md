### 2025/04/22  
Today, while fixing the NoticeBar component of my blog, I looked at my blog and felt quite satisfied with the time and effort I’ve spent on deployment to achieve its current state.  

However, after staring at it for a while, I couldn’t help but feel that my blog isn’t particularly outstanding—it always seems like others’ blog frameworks are much better.  

![Blog](https://imgse.koxiuqiu.cc/imgse/u/23/2025/04/23/6807c19ade58b.png)  

But the path is my own, the deployment was done step by step with my own hands,  
the plugins were written from scratch,  
and the theme was configured bit by bit.  
From nothing to something,  
from cluelessness to being able to write plugins freely—  
isn’t this progress in itself?  

Moreover, my article ["Building Your Own Blog with Valaxy"](https://www.xscnas.top/posts/p1120250403) still receives many questions from fellow bloggers, which truly surprised me.  
I thought of myself as a nobody, unlikely to attract much attention, but my tutorial video on Bilibili has already surpassed 1,000 views!  
Seeing this data made me incredibly happy because my efforts haven’t been in vain!  
I’ll keep striving to do better!  

## Website Updates  

### Fixing the NoticeBar Component  
It’s already early morning on 2025/03/23, and I’ve just finished fixing the NoticeBar component because the IP detection API went down! (I never expected the API I chose to fail so quickly.)  
It barely lasted a little over a month before dying—ToT.  
So, after careful consideration, I switched to the API from [https://api.nsmao.net/](https://api.nsmao.net/). Though it’s also a free API, the fact that it has an ICP备案 (Chinese internet content provider license) gives me hope that it’ll last longer.  
Also—a huge thanks to all the sites offering free APIs.  
In 2025, providing free services is something very few are willing to do...
