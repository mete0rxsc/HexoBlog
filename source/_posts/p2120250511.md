> That day, 12 young soccer players and their coach went missing while exploring the Tham Luang Nang Non cave in Chiang Rai, Thailand. They were later found trapped inside the cave. From June 23 to July 10, what began as a local disappearance case turned into an international rescue operation, ultimately saving all 13 trapped individuals.  

All 13 trapped individuals were successfully rescued, but tragically, two rescuers lost their lives in the process.
